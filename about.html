About Our Company

Head Space Productions works throughout the Pacific Northwest to create an immersive audiovisual experience, including music and performance art.

Our Mission

As a production company, Head Space LLC aspires to provide a safe environment for artists of all different media, and people from all walks of life to come together and share their art and ideas in a conscious and positive way. To achieve this, we intend to promote artists and events that inspire transformational experiences among participants and members of global communities.

This company is the result of a collaborative effort by a close community for the larger community. When you are really serious about something you make it yourself.
